Join the citizens of Lion's Arch as they enjoy the Dragon Bash festival, a month-long celebration packed with fireworks, all-new activities, and exclusive rewards!

Festival Activities

The Captain's Council of Lion's Arch has spared no expense in decorating the city for the Dragon Bash, as evidenced by the giant holographic dragon soaring overhead. Join in the festivities by testing your skill in the new Dragon Ball mini-game and your luck in the all-new Moa Racing activity! Hunt down holographic dragons and dragon effigies all over Tyria to show your dragon-bashing spirit.

Dragon Bash Fireworks

Every night in-game, the skies will light up with a celebratory fireworks display. The show starts at 9AM PDT on Friday, June 14 and will go off every two hours until 12PM PDT on Monday, June 17.

Burn the Dragon

Join the citizens of Lion's Arch as they light dragon effigies in the Grand Piazza starting June 18. Hosted by the jovial Magnus the Bloody Handed, this ceremony will be full of merriment and surprises.

Exclusive Rewards

No celebration is complete without prizes; you'll be able to earn festival items like the unique Horns of the Dragon helm, exclusive Holographic Dragon Wings back items, and Dragon Coffers packed with special loot.
